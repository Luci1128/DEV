Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology

Sir M. Visvesvaraya Institute of Technology (Sir MVIT) is a well-regarded engineering college in Bengaluru, established in 1986, known for strong placements, an extensive campus, and various B.E., M.Tech, MBA, and MCA programs, affiliated with VTU and approved by AICTE, offering good infrastructure and facilities for holistic student development.

Undergraduate (B.E.) Departments:

  • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE): Including specializations like Data Science and IoT & Cybersecurity.HOD
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE).
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE).
  • Information Science & Engineering (ISE).
  • Mechanical Engineering.
  • Civil Engineering.
  • Biotechnology.
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML).
  • Robotics & Artificial Intelligence.
  • Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering (E&TC).
  • School of Architecture.
